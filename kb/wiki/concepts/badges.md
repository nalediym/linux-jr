## Definition
Three act-level badges that each encode a layer of the ethos: **Scout** (Act 1), **Helper** (Act 2), **White Hat** (Act 3).
— [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)#c-d20b593c, [ethos.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--ethos.md.md)#c-2c3eaa21

## Details
- **Scout (Act 1):** observer who asks first — you find things and ask before acting.
- **Helper (Act 2):** builds and fixes — always with permission.
- **White Hat (Act 3):** the full ethical hacker — curious, clever, kind.
— [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)#c-d20b593c, [ethos.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--ethos.md.md)#c-2c3eaa21, [act-1.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-1.md.md)#c-4751f156, [act-2.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-2.md.md)#c-d5451ecd, [act-3.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-3.md.md)#c-17e1825f

An earlier draft used the names **Explorer / Tinker / Workshop Key**. ethos.md explicitly renames these so every badge reinforces the ethos.
