## Definition
The downstairs sub-area of the workshop: cool, slightly dusty, housing a maze of old shelving units left over from when the workshop was a shop. Hosts M3.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-b359f32f

## Details
Canonical filesystem shape: `basement/entrance/` is the maze start, with branches named `[left, right, down]` per setting.md. M3's implemented path uses `entrance` → `left` → `down` → `right` → `down` → `.secret/` (containing `pip.txt` with `FLAG{pip_found}` and a `cookies.txt`).
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-b359f32f, [mission-03](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-03-the-maze.js.md)#c-d4620c7d

The maze is where M3's `cd ..` introduction happens — dead ends (spider; old socks) force the kid to go back.
— [act-1.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-1.md.md)#c-b5d8cbe1, [mission-03](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-03-the-maze.js.md)#c-d4620c7d

**Never exists:** basement sub-levels past the maze.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-9c076fc9

Pip takes Sprocket down there in M3 — the retrofit that gave Act 1 a Sprocket through-line.
— [act-1.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-1.md.md)#c-b5d8cbe1, [mission-03](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-03-the-maze.js.md)#c-d4620c7d
