## Definition
Tinker-mentor / patient-uncle-figure. "Captain" is Pip's affectionate nickname, NOT a military rank. Rex is the adult who owns the workshop. Signature phrase "Let's have a look." UI color #4a8aff steady blue.
— [captain-rex.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--captain-rex.yaml.md)#c-963e0626, c-35b48b21, c-656558fd

## Details
Want/need gap: wants a quiet afternoon for his own project; needs to let Pip and the player help (delegate, trust).
— [captain-rex.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--captain-rex.yaml.md)#c-22ef10c3

Speech: finishes sentences, uses "Mm" a lot, "Well now" when surprised, never raises his voice. Measured pace, warm-low pitch.
— [captain-rex.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--captain-rex.yaml.md)#c-35b48b21

**Canon:** owns the workshop (kitchen, garage, study, basement). His computer got locked by Pip in M2 — reframed in the act-1 arc doc as Rex forgot his own password (consensual help). Has a study with a bookshelf ("rockets, dogs, Binary for Beginners") and a desk drawer with a photo of Pip and a friend at the beach.
— [captain-rex.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--captain-rex.yaml.md)#c-49366ca0, [act-1.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-1.md.md)#c-512c914e, [mission-02](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-02-the-secret-code.js.md)#c-102a9614

**Role:** gives every mission briefing ("Captain Rex's log, entry…"), provides gentle hints, owns the resolution reward ("Nice work"), models the ethos by asking permission, saying thank you, and gently correcting Pip.

The hat Rex wears every day was made by his kid — "which kid" is deliberately unspecified.
