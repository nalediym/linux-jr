# Deferred Commands (not in Season 1)

> **First seen in:** [act-3.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-3.md.md)
> **Also referenced by:** [commands.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--commands.md.md), [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md), [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)
> **Confidence:** high (4 sources agree)
> **Aliases:** not-in-Season-1, Season 2+ commands

## Definition
Commands and concepts explicitly held out of Season 1 — either because they're too abstract for age 7, too risky for Nick Jr. safety, or violate a world-building rule.
— [act-3.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-3.md.md)#c-67be01e9

## Details
- **`rm` / recycle bin** — pending decision; Nick Jr. register makes delete nervous. Possible "recycle" substitute. — [act-3.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-3.md.md)#c-67be01e9, c-a953819b
- **Piping (`|`)** — too abstract for age 7 per act-3.md. educational.md also lists "pipe" as a banned vocabulary word except as a later-season metaphor. — [act-3.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-3.md.md)#c-67be01e9, [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md)#c-ca588740
- **`chmod` / permissions** — too abstract; deferred to Season 2. — [act-3.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-3.md.md)#c-67be01e9, [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md)#c-ca588740
- **Network / internet metaphors** — violate the no-internet setting rule. — [act-3.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-3.md.md)#c-67be01e9
- **Jargon banned at the vocab level:** syscall, kernel, shell, stdin, stdout, exit code, process. — [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md)#c-ca588740, [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)#c-b795fdab

Commands that *are* in Season 1 (for contrast): `pwd`, `ls`, `cd`, `cd ..`, `cat`, `mkdir`, `touch`, `echo >`, `echo >>`, `help`, `man`.
— [commands.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--commands.md.md)#c-c6ef427f
