## Definition
The end-of-Season-1 test that proves Linux Jr worked: the kid opens Terminal.app on a real Mac, types `pwd`, and smiles.
— [commands.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--commands.md.md)#c-61a9e221

## Details
This is framed as "the whole product succeeding." It's the success metric for the Heart concept: if the kid can graduate to a real shell with no unlearning required, Linux Jr did its job.
— [commands.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--commands.md.md)#c-61a9e221, c-c97c193b

Inspiration is drawn from real-vs-toy comparisons: Duolingo (real language not fake), piano lessons (real piano not toy piano), bike with training wheels (still a real bike).
— [commands.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--commands.md.md)#c-61a9e221

The Never-compromise commitments back this up: typing is mandatory, the player graduates.
— [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)#c-77916829, [commands.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--commands.md.md)#c-f1f3317e
