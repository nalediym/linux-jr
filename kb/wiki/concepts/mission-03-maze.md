## Definition
The third mission. Pip took Sprocket down to the basement and got turned around. The player follows note-clues through the maze, goes back when they hit dead ends, and finds Pip in a hidden `.secret/` room with cookies.
— [act-1.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-1.md.md)#c-b5d8cbe1, [mission-03](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-03-the-maze.js.md)#c-d4620c7d

## Details
- **Character focus:** Pip.
- **Track A commands:** introduces `cd ..`; reinforces nested `cd` and dotfiles.
- **Track B concept:** *sequencing* — "order matters: step by step."
- **Vocabulary:** parent (folder), back, nested, step.
- **Real-world callback:** "Recipes, dance moves, even brushing your teeth — they're all sequences."
