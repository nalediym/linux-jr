## Definition
Kid-inventor friend. Curious, impulsive, earnest. Signature phrase "Oh! Oh! Watch this!" and thinking-aloud "Okay okay okay —". UI color #ffd84a sunny yellow.
— [pip.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--pip.yaml.md)#c-9b5eb47b, c-53a2d278, c-0bcf7812

## Details
Want/need gap: wants to finish inventing right now; needs to ask for help and slow down.
— [pip.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--pip.yaml.md)#c-7381d76a

Three-layer identity: public = "the kid who always has a new gadget"; private = "worries the inventions won't actually work"; core = "wants Captain Rex and the player to be proud of what they build."
— [pip.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--pip.yaml.md)#c-57137311

**Canon through M1–M3:** hides a secret blueprint in a secret-room dotfile (M1); the robot-cat blueprint IS Sprocket (M1); leaves sticky notes as clues (M2); wanders into the basement and gets lost in the maze (M3); eats cookies while waiting to be found (M3).
— [pip.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--pip.yaml.md)#c-7527fb1c, [mission-01](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-01-find-the-flag.js.md)#c-407ad082, [mission-02](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-02-the-secret-code.js.md)#c-102a9614, [mission-03](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-03-the-maze.js.md)#c-d4620c7d

Pip is the teachable-moment vehicle for the ethos — occasionally forgets to ask first, gets a gentle Rex correction. Max once per act.

Pip carries most of Act 1's hooks: M1 (lost blueprint), M3 (got turned around in maze), and recurs in Act 2 (M4 room, M6 welcome sign) and Act 3 (M7 todo notes, M9 Sprocket ears).
