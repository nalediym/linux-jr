## Definition
Pip's half-built robot-cat sidekick. Only beeps — never speaks. Signature "Beep-boop!". UI color #ff66cc bubblegum pink. Born from Pip's M1 blueprint.
— [sprocket.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--sprocket.yaml.md)#c-469acef1, c-2507fc2e, c-62343ab6, c-c92a975b

## Details
Want/need gap: wants to follow Pip everywhere (even into the dryer); needs a consistent off-switch to actually rest.
— [sprocket.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--sprocket.yaml.md)#c-d1dbe8de

**Canon extension:** the M1 blueprint has been built — but only partially. Ears don't quite work. Tail falls off. Purrs by beeping.
