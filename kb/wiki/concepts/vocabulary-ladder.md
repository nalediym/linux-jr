# Vocabulary Ladder

> **First seen in:** [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)
> **Also referenced by:** [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md), [act-1.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-1.md.md)

## Definition
A staged list of 2–5 new words introduced per mission so that by M10 the kid has a working computing vocabulary — without ever using jargon that's too abstract for age 7.
— [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md)#c-8630f198

## Details
- **Act 1:** directory, file, path, hidden, flag, root, parent (folder), listing.
- **Act 2:** create, empty file, write, contents, marker, structure.
- **Act 3:** search, organize, combine, project, command, help.
— [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)#c-b795fdab, [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md)#c-8630f198

**Banned words (too jargony for age 7):** syscall, kernel, shell, stdin, stdout, exit code, pipe (except as metaphor in later seasons), permissions (deferred to Season 2), process.
— [educational.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--educational.md.md)#c-ca588740, [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)#c-b795fdab

M1's vocab of directory/file/path/hidden/flag aligns to the ladder.
— [act-1.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--arcs--act-1.md.md)#c-d18daf3e
