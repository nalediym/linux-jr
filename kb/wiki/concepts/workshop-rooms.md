## Definition
The catalog of individual rooms in the workshop (excluding basement, covered by [basement-maze](basement-maze.md)). Each room has implied content that writers should preserve across missions.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-b359f32f
