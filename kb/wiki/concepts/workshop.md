## Definition
The single shared setting of Linux Jr: a cozy, slightly-messy tinker-workshop and home owned by Captain Rex. Pip, Rex, and Sprocket live there; the player is a visiting friend.
— [story.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--story.yaml.md)#c-d8c1c07c, [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-b03687cb

## Details
**Vibe:** Roald Dahl's chocolate factory × a grandparent's garage × a Pixar short. Always warm lighting. Sawdust, cocoa, oil, cookies. Safe — never actually dangerous.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-f9ab0770

**Canon rooms (M1–M3):** workshop/ (toolbox, kitchen, garage, garage/workbench, study, study/bookshelf, secret-room) plus basement/ (entrance + maze branches).
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-b359f32f, [mission-01](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-01-find-the-flag.js.md)#c-407ad082, [mission-02](../sources/src--data--missions--mission-02-the-secret-code.js.md)#c-102a9614

**Expansion rooms (M4–M10):** attic, roof (telescope), garden, garden/shed, garden/greenhouse, neighbor, mailbox. Closed set — don't invent new rooms.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-e4048ebd

**Recurring props:** Pip's toolbox (labeled drawers, some wrong), Rex's logbook, Sprocket's detachable tail, the cookie jar (kitchen), Rex's hat, a red button for an Act 3 reveal.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-5f41838b

**Never exists:** basement sub-levels past the maze, second floor (just attic ladder from garage), off-world locations, dangerous places.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-9c076fc9

**Time:** always warm afternoon or early evening. No night scenes, no storms.
— [setting.md](../sources/linux-jr-story--world--setting.md.md)#c-f9ab0770

Owner context: Rex owns it and lets Pip run wild in it.
— [captain-rex.yaml](../sources/linux-jr-story--characters--captain-rex.yaml.md)#c-963e0626, c-49366ca0
